Zaina Bouzerrade (; born 29 July 2002) is a Dutch-born Moroccan footballer who plays as a right-back for Eredivisie club Ajax and the Morocco women's national team.

Club career
Bouzerrade is a VVIJ IJsselstein product. She has played for Ajax in the Netherlands.
