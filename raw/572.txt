Olympiacos Football Club B, or simply Olympiacos B (Greek: Ολυμπιακός Β), is the reserve team of Greek club Olympiacos. It is set to premiere in the Super League Greece 2 in the 2021–22 season.

Stadium
The stadium that hosts in Olympiacos is the Rentis Training Centre with a capacity of 3,000.

Coach and staff
Ariel Ibagaza was appointed the coach in July 2021, with former Nottingham Forest youth coach Guilherme Ramos appointed his assistant in August.
