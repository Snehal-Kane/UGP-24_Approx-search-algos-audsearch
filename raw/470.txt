Inès Arouaissa (; born 30 June 2001) is a French-born Moroccan footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Division 2 Féminine club Olympique de Marseille and the Morocco women's national team.

Club career 
Arouaissa is a Marseille product, which she joined in 2016.

International career
Arouaissa made her senior debut for Morocco on 10 June 2021 in a 3–0 friendly home win over Mali.
