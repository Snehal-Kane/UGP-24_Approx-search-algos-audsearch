Ionuț Andrei Moisă (born 7 February 2002) is a Romanian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Liga II side Politehnica Iași. He made his Liga I debut on 18 January 2021, in a match between Politehnica Iași and FC Voluntari, won by Botoșani, score 1–0.
