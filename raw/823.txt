The 2021 Arab Athletics Championships was the twenty second edition of the international athletics competition between Arab countries that took place from 16–20 June 2021 at Rades Athletic Stadium in Radès, close to Tunis, the capital of Tunisia. Around 400 athletes from 18 nations attended the event.
