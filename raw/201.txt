Moji Solar-Wilson (born in 1968) from Ijero-Ekiti in Ekiti State, is a Nigerian UK-based real estate broker, LGBTQIA+ activist, social commentator, and the CEO of Solar Worldwide Realty Inc. In 2017, she married her lesbian partner, Margaret Wilson, and became the first know Nigerian lesbian couple.
