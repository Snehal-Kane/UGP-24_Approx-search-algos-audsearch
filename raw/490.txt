Éva Allice (; born 2 January 2002) is a French-born Moroccan footballer who plays as a defender for Division 3 Féminine club FC Nantes and the Morocco women's national team.

Club career 
Allice has played for Le Mans FC and Nantes in France.

International career
Allice made her senior debut for Morocco on 10 June 2021 in a 3–0 friendly home win over Mali.
