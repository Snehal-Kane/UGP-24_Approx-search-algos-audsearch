Yasmina Laaroussi (; born 15 February 1994) is a Moroccan footballer who plays as a midfielder for Swiss Women's Super League club Servette FC and the Morocco women's national team.

Club career 
Laaroussi has played for Servette in Switzerland.

International career
Laaroussi made her senior debut for Morocco on 14 June 2021 in a 3–2 friendly home win over Mali.
