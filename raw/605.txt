Ghizlane Chhiri (; born 11 September 1994) is a Moroccan footballer who plays as a defender for ASFAR and the Morocco women's national team.

Club career
Chhiri has played for ASFAR in Morocco.

International career
Chhiri has capped for Morocco at senior level.
