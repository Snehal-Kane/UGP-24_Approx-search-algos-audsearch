Jeyson Ariel Chura Almanza (born 3 February 2002), is a Bolivian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Bolivian Primera División side The Strongest.

International career
He was selected for Bolivia squad for the 2021 Copa América and made his debut on 18 June 2021 in a game against Chile.
