Charles "Scottie" Duncan, nicknamed "Tall Papa and Trees", is an American former Negro league pitcher who played between 1937 and 1940.


Duncan made his Negro leagues debut in 1937 with the Pittsburgh Crawfords He went on to play for the Birmingham Black Barons and Atlanta Black Crackers, and finished his career in 1940 with the New Orleans–St.
 Louis Stars.
