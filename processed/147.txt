Ankaromalaza Mifanasoa is a town and commune in Madagascar.
 It belongs to the district of Vohibato, which is a part of Haute Matsiatra Region.
 The population of the commune was estimated to be 7720 in 2018.


Primary and junior level secondary education are available in town The majority 99.

  The most important crop is rice, while other important products are beans, cassava and sweet potatoes.
