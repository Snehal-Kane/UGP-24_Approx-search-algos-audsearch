Martinsdale Colony is a Hutterite community and census-designated place (CDP) in Wheatland County, Montana, United States.
 It is on the western edge of the county, bordered to the west by Meagher County and  northeast of the unincorporated community of Martinsdale.
 The Musselshell River forms the southern edge of the CDP.


US.
 Route 12 runs through the southern part of the CDP, leading east  to Harlowton, the Wheatland county seat, and west  to White Sulphur Springs.


The community was first listed as a CDP prior to the 2020 census
