Alin Constantin Damian (born 11 April 1994) is a Romanian professional footballer who plays as a forward.
 In his career, Damian also played for teams such as FC Brașov, Unirea Tărlungeni, SR Brașov or Olimpic Cetate Râșnov, among others.
 He also played in the Norwegian fifth tier for Emblem IL.
