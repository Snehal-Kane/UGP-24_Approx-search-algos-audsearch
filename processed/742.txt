ReforMers () is a Hungarian centre-right to right-wing political party founded by Andrea Varga-Damm, a former member of Jobbik.


History

On 24 July 2020, Andrea Varga-Damm was expelled from Jobbik On 21 May 2021, Varga-Damm founded her own political party, which she named the ReforMers.
