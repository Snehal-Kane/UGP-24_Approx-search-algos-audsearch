Tõnu Saar (born on 6 August 1944 Väljaotsa, Kuremaa Parish) is an Estonian actor.


In 1972 he graduated from the Tallinn State Conservatory's Performing Arts Department From 1963 until 1968, he worked as a photographer.
 From 1972 until 1980, he worked at Estonian Youth Theatre, and from 1980 until 1998, at Estonian Drama Theatre.
 Besides theatre roles he has appeared in a number of films and on television.
