Raul Ioan Palmeș (born 16 August 1996) is a Romanian professional footballer who plays as a defender for Liga II side Concordia Chiajna.
 In his career, Palmeș also played for teams such as Budapest Honvéd, Kazincbarcikai SC or FK Miercurea Ciuc.
