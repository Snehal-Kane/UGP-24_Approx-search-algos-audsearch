Adolpho Millon Júnior (16 September 1895 – 7 May 1929), known as just Millon, was a Brazilian footballer.
 He played in six matches for the Brazil national football team from 1914 to 1919.
 He was also part of Brazil's squad for the 1917 South American Championship.
