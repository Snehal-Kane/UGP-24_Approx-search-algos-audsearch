COVID-19 vaccination in Eritrea is an ongoing immunisation campaign against severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2), the virus that causes coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19), in response to the ongoing pandemic in the country.


COVID-19 vaccination has not begun in Eritrea as of July 2021
