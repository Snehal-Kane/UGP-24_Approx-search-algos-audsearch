Cosmin Saizu (born 8 March 2003) is a Romanian professional footballer who plays as a defender for Liga II side Politehnica Iași.
 He made his Liga I debut on 7 November 2020, in a match between FC Voluntari and Politehnica Iași, won by Voluntari, score 4–0.
