Lamosina is a village and commune in Madagascar.
 It belongs to the district of Vohibato, which is a part of Haute Matsiatra Region.
 The population of the commune was estimated to be 11534 in 2018.

Naming 
Lamosina (literally "back") had its name because it is the extreme village seen from the roof [tafo] of the rock, roof that is like a bump or a back lamosina
