Biography 
Stone Deavours Barefield was born on July 28, 1927, in Laurel, Mississippi He was the son of Samuel Stephen Barefield, Sr.
 and Dinah (Deavours) Barefield.
 He graduated from Hattiesburg High School in 1945.
 He graduated from the University of Southern Mississippi with a bachelor's degree in 1952, and then graduated from the University of Mississippi School of Law.
 He became a licensed attorney in 1954.
 He was a member of the Mississippi House of Representatives from 1960 to 1984.
 He died unexpectedly at his house in Hattiesburg, Mississippi, on March 20, 2009.
