Camrose Colony is a Hutterite community and census-designated place (CDP) in Toole County, Montana, United States.
 It is in the southern part of the county,  southeast of Shelby, the Toole county seat, and  northeast of Conrad.


Camrose Colony was first listed as a CDP prior to the 2020 census
