Jan Zabłocki ( – ?) was a Polish major of the 12 Crown Pedestrian Regiment (infantry unit of the military of the Polish–Lithuanian Commonwealth), participant in the Polish–Russian War of 1792, decorated with the war order of Virtuti Militari.
