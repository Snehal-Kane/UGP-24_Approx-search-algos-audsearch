Akudo Oguaghamba is a Nigerian human rights activist and educator.
 She has published articles on LGBTQ+ and gender rights.
 She's the founder for Women's Health and Equal Rights WHER and was also a co-chair (Female) of PAN-AFRICAN ILGA (International Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Trans and Intersex Association).
