Club Balonmano Ramón y Cajal is a Spanish multi-sports club based in the autonomous city of Ceuta.
 Founded in 2003, their football team plays in Regional Preferente de Ceuta, holding home games at Estadio José Martínez Pirri, with a capacity of 1,000 people.
