The English women's cricket team toured Australia and New Zealand in November 2000.
 They played Australia women's national cricket team in 4 One Day Internationals and New Zealand in 5 One Day Internationals.
 They lost both series, losing 4–0 to Australia and 5–0 to New Zealand.
