Zeph E.
 Daniel (formerly known as Woody Keith) is a screenwriter, film director, and producer.
 He is known for conceiving and co-writing the 1989 American body horror film Society, as well as the 1990 film Bride of Re-Animator, with Rick Fry.
 Daniel also wrote Silent Night, Deadly Night 4: Initiation (1990), and served as a producer and writer on the 2021 film Girl Next.
