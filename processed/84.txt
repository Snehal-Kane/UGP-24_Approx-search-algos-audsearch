Katherine Cadwallader Douglass is an American swimmer.
  In 2021 she placed second in the 200m individual medley at the 2020 Olympic Swimming Trials, qualifying for the US Olympic Team.
  She is presently a member of the US National Swimming Team.
 She previously was named to the US National Junior Team, competing in the Youth Olympic Games in 2018 and the Junior World Championships in 2017.
  She is from Pelham, New York.
