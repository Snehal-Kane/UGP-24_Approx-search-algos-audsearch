Sam Tanner (born 24 August 2000) is a New Zealand athlete.


A former surfer, he set a national indoor 1500m record of 3:3474 in February 2020 to secure the automatic Olympic qualification mark at Staten Island, New York He was confirmed on the New Zealand team for the delayed 2020 Tokyo Olympics in April 2021.
 

Tanner is Māori; his iwi affiliation is Ngāpuhi
