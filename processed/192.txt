Isaka, Ambohimahasoa is a rural commune in the  Central Highlands of Madagascar.
 It belongs to the district of Ambohimahasoa, which is a part of Haute Matsiatra Region.
 The population of the commune was estimated to be 11,642 in 2018.


Only primary schooling is available The majority 98% of the population of the commune are farmers.
  The most important crops are rice and potatoes, while other important agricultural products are peanuts and cassava.
 Services provide employment for 2% of the population.
