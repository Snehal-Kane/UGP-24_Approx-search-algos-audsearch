The Albania Billie Jean King Cup team represents Albania in Billie Jean King Cup tennis competition and are governed by the Albanian Tennis Federation.
 They currently compete in the Europe/Africa Zone of Group III.


History
Albania competed in its first Billie Jean King Cup in 2021 Their best result was finishing third in their Group III pool in 2021.
