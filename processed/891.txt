Sylvio Vidal Leite Ribeiro (born 1 October 1896, date of death unknown), known simply as Vidal, was a Brazilian footballer.
 He played in four matches for the Brazil national football team in 1917.
 He was also part of Brazil's squad for the 1917 South American Championship.
