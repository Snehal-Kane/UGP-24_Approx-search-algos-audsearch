Unión Deportiva Guía is a Spanish football club based in Santa María de Guía, Gran Canaria, in the Canary Islands.
 Founded in 1958, it plays in Preferente Interinsular – Group 2, holding home games at Estadio Octavio Estevez de Guía de Gran Canaria, with a capacity of 1,000 people.


Season to season

2 seasons in Tercera División
