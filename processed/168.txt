Hillside Colony is a Hutterite community and census-designated place (CDP) in Toole County, Montana, United States.
 It is in the northwest corner of the county,  west of Interstate 15 at Sweet Grass and  south of the Canadian border.


Hillside Colony was first listed as a CDP prior to the 2020 census
