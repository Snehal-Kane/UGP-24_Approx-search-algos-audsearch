Kuda Bank formerly called Kudi money is a Nigerian free digital-only bank that was founded in 2017, is a  London-based startup operating with a microfinance license and mobile banking services, it was renamed Kuda Bank in 2019.
 It was founded by Babs Ogundeyi and Musty Mustapha.


Kuda Bank is known for not charging bank fees and earned significant support from the 2020 End SARS protesters due to their contributions to the protests

The bank awarded its millionth customer with 1million Naira It was listed as one of the seven WEF African technology startups of 2021.
