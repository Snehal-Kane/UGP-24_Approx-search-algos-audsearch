Dorjan Bajrami (born 2 March 1996) is a retired Albanian  professional football player.
 He spent the majority of his career at his hometown club Turbina, as well as playing for Gramshi, Luzi and Bylis.
 He announced his retirement from football at the age of 25 on 16 June 2021.
