Argyripnus atlanticus is a species of ray-finned fish in the genus Argyripnus.


The fish is found worldwide in the tropical waters of the Eastern Atlantic, from Madeira to south of the Canary Islands In the Western Atlantic, in the Bahamas and the Caribbean Sea.
 In the Eastern Central Pacific, it is found in Hawaii.
